Forecast Accuracy

100 - (|Actual - Forecast| / Actual × 100)

Measures how closely the forecast matched actual demand.

Weighted SLA

Σ(Actual SLA × Answered) / Σ(Answered)

Gives more weight to intervals that handled more contacts.

AHT Deviation

Average Actual AHT - Average Target AHT

Shows whether handling time is above or below target.

Staffing Gap

Actual HC - Required HC

Negative means understaffing. Positive means over-coverage.

Volume Variance %

((Actual Volume - Forecast Volume) / Forecast Volume) × 100

Measures interval demand spike or softness against plan.

AHT Variance %

((Actual AHT - Target AHT) / Target AHT) × 100

Measures interval handling-time deterioration or improvement.

Backlog Opening

Previous Interval Closing Backlog

Carried-forward unfinished workload from the prior interval.

Backlog Closing

max(0, Opening Backlog + Actual Volume - Answered)

Approximates remaining demand after handled workload.

Estimated Capacity

(Actual HC × Interval Seconds) / Actual AHT

Approximates how many contacts available staff could handle in the interval.

Severity

Based on SLA miss, HC gap %, backlog growth, and AHT / volume pressure

Used to classify each interval into low, medium, or high severity.

Confidence

Single strong signal = High, mixed signals = Medium, weak signal = Low

Indicates how strongly the data supports the assigned RCA.

What-If Required HC

(Actual Volume × Volume Multiplier × Actual AHT × AHT Multiplier) / Interval Seconds

Shows how staffing requirement changes under simulated conditions.
